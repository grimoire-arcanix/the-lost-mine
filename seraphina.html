Character Chronicle

Seraphina Thumbleton

The survivor who turned betrayal into influence

Seraphina came to Phandalin lured by rumors of quick wealth and careless settlers. She did not arrive seeking honest labor. Instead, she slipped into the orbit of the Redbrands, where burglary, fencing, and intimidation paid in coin and secrecy. She lived by instinct, quick hands, and the sort of luck that smiles only briefly.

That life changed when someone inside the gang betrayed her. Marked for death and nearly killed under the orders of Glasstaff, Seraphina fled with little but her tools and Tymora’s favor. She returned not because she believed in redemption, but because unfinished business has a way of calling the wounded home. During the campaign, she outwitted goblins, earned the dagger of Klarg, exposed Lady Hilda, and survived the layered deceptions surrounding the Black Spider. Again and again, her instincts kept the party alive in places where blunt strength alone would have failed.

In the aftermath, Halia Thornton saw clearly what Seraphina had become: not merely a survivor, but a woman capable of shaping the hidden currents of a region. Seraphina entered the Zhentarim, rose through the Black Network, and took control of the smuggling routes once left behind by Hale Hilda. Where once she survived on the edges of someone else’s scheme, she now moved in the quiet center of power, dealing in shadows with a confidence hard-earned and never given freely.
